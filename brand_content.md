1. **Identify a High-Demand Problem**: Great businesses start with problems, not products.
2. **Create a Powerful, Simple Offer**: Simple to explain, fast to deliver results.
3. **Build Trust Through Daily Content**: Education as the primary authority builder.
4. **Use Digital Marketing**: Predictable engines like Meta Ads and Google Ads.
5. **Customer Experience System**: Management and follow-up systems.
6. **Delegate & Build a Team**: Focus on strategy while delegating low-level tasks.
7. **Adopting the 7-Figure Mindset**: Consistency and ownership.
